Stroke Information

What is a Stroke?

A stroke occurs when there is a disruption of blood flow to the brain, resulting in damage to brain cells.

Symptoms

  • Sudden numbness or weakness in the face, arm, or leg
  • Confusion or trouble speaking
  • Severe headache with no known cause
  • Trouble walking or loss of balance

Risk Factors

  • High blood pressure
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Family history of stroke

What You Can Do

  • Seek emergency medical attention if you suspect a stroke
  • Control blood pressure and manage other risk factors
  • Adopt a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet
  • Follow medical advice for stroke prevention
