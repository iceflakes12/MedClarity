Kidney Stones Information

What are Kidney Stones?

Kidney stones are hard deposits that form in the kidneys when there is an imbalance of substances, such as calcium, oxalate, and phosphorus, in the urine.

Symptoms

  • Severe pain in the back or side
  • Pain radiating to the lower abdomen and groin
  • Blood in the urine
  • Frequent urination

Causes and Risk Factors

  • Dehydration
  • High dietary intake of certain minerals
  • Family history of kidney stones
  • Medical conditions affecting urine concentration

What You Can Do

  • Drinking plenty of water to stay hydrated
  • Pain medications for relief
  • Medical procedures to break up or remove larger stones
  • Dietary modifications to prevent recurrence
